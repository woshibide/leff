Programme 2026 coming soon

19—22 September 2026

Cinema Kijkhuis & Museum De Lakenhal

Thank you for visiting our website. The first edition of the Leiden Essay Film Festival held in September 2025 was a tremendous success. More than 1200 visitors attended and we received countless enthusiastic responses. Since then we have regularly been notified about new essay films and visitors keep asking us when the next edition of the festival will take place. We’ll be able to share more information about this soon.
