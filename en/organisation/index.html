Organisation

leff is a joint initiative. Born from an idea by the Academy of Creative and Performing Arts at Leiden University, the Discursive Film Foundation was established, joined by the FILM lectoraat of the Royal Conservatoire The Hague and ReCNTR (which focuses on the integration of image and sound in the sciences). leff is a partner and part of the Days of Art & Science. We Are Public, Cineville, LIFF (Leiden International Film Festival), and Leiden Global are partners in reaching our audience.
