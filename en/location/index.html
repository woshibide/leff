Cinema Kijkhuis, 15 and 16 September 2025

Vrouwenkerksteeg 10 2312 WS Leiden

Public Transport

Both Museum De Lakenhal and the Kijkhuis are within a 10-minute walk from Leiden Central Station.

By Car

The nearest parking garage is Lammermarkt Parking Garage (Lammermarkt 20P, 2312 CW Leiden). By booking a parking ticket online, you can park at a reduced rate and guarantee a parking space.
